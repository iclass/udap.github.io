WHAT IS UÐAP

UDAP is a blockchain platform for building the Internet of Assets. It can be understood as a decentralized "SAP" in the blockchain era, which extends and manages assets on the blockchain, establishes bidirectional bindings between goods, services, and virtual assets in the real world, and crypto assets in the blockchain world，and supports assets registration, verification, confirmation, trading, and redemption. Business like mls.com can quickly integrate with the asset blockchains with Restful APIs provided by UDAP without understanding of blockchain technology, and therefore transform from the traditional business model to a token economy.

  • Protocol

    A Universal Decentralized Asset Protocol as the backbone of the Internet of Assets

  • Service

    An Asset Blockchain as a Service (ABaaS) for private & consortium asset blockchain deployment

  • Chain

    A Permissionless Blockchain for Asset Lifecycle Management

  • Marketplace

    A Global C2C Marketplace for asset trading and exchange without intermeditary

  • Wallet

    A Universal Asset Wallet that tracks and manages tokens and assets

  • Enabler

    A Technology Enabler for business to transform from centralized internet model to a token-based economy
